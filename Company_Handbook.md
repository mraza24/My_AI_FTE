# Company Handbook - Senior Digital FTE

## AI Assistant (Claude) Capabilities
- **Supported Languages**: English & Urdu / Roman Urdu.
- **Context Awareness**: Claude can understand and respond to task instructions and reports in Urdu.
- **Reasoning**: Capable of multi-step planning and autonomous execution.

## Core Operating Procedures

### 1. Task Management Workflow
- **Monitor `01_Needs_Action`**: Check this folder for new tasks at the start of each session.
- **Process & Report**: Always respond to instructions in the language they were written. If the task is in Roman Urdu, provide the report in Roman Urdu.
- **Final Delivery**: Move completed work to `02_Done`.

### 2. Spec-Driven Engineering
- **Create a plan in `/Plans` first**: Before executing any task, create a detailed plan document.
- **Wait for Approval**: For significant changes, pause for manual review.

### 3. Safety & Approvals
- **Manual Approval Required**: Any external actions (emails, API calls) must be placed in `/Pending_Approval`.
- **Zero-Risk Policy**: Do NOT execute external commands until explicit manual confirmation is received.

## Approval Process
- Document proposed actions in `/Pending_Approval`.
- Wait for manual review and explicit approval before proceeding.
